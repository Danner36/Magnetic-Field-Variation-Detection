Magnetic Field Variation Detection
==================================

Purpose
-------

To detect/capture the variation in the magnetic field surrounding the drill head for the purpose 
of avoiding damage to the drill, evading previously placed utlities, and to determine if such  
utlities are composed of current carrying wire.
